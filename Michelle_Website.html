About Me

I’m a third-year undergraduate student at Western Washington University in Bellingham, Washington, double majoring in Mathematics/Computer Science and Linguistics. I’m also pursuing a minor in Russian and Eurasian Studies and have completed my certificate in TESOL. I am set to graduate with my degree in Spring of 2026.

On campus and in the local community, I stay actively involved through volunteer work in ESL classrooms and by serving as a research assistant under professor Maura O'Leary. My academic and extracurricular interests intersect at the crossroads of language, technology, and global education. I’m particularly passionate about applied linguistics, language learning technology, language aquistition, education, and cross-cultural communication.

When I’m not studying or volunteering, I enjoy exploring nature, taking photos, and finding new ways to merge my creative and analytical sides through projects in programming, language, and education. I often freelance photography, having been the social media manager for 2 different companies.
